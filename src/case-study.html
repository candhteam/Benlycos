01. Live Streaming

Experience Seamless Live Streaming With Zifilink

Online live streaming is the new way to broadcast real-time events and activities over the internet. With audiences rapidly shifting towards online video streaming platforms from television, internet is the new way to go for hosting live video streaming. One of the primary requisites of an online livestream- fast and stable internet connection.

This is where many corporates and independent content creators face the main challenge. Relying on one broadband connection is never a wise idea when you are live streaming an important event. With lags and buffers, there is always the risk of losing audiences’ interests.

Zifilink has actively assessed this issue and has helped numerous corporates and event management agencies with a professional live streaming solution. With Zifilink’s link aggregation services, live broadcasters have been able to connect with up to 8 different networks for unmatched internet speed and reliability.

Thus, when one connection suffers a failover, the other available connections step in and provide the same speed of internet, without a buffer. In other words, when connections suffer from failovers, the livestream is absolutely unaffected and neither the host nor the audiences can spot an error. This is a staple of a professional livestream which numerous organizations have been able to accomplish with Zifilink.

Challenges

The following are the main challenges faced while hosting an online livestream:

  • A truly high-speed internet connection
  • A reliable internet connection with zero failover risks
  • Video quality of the livestream.
Solution

Zifilink’s internet bonding device has been able to eliminate all the primary problems that streamers face during live streaming a video over the internet:

  • Max internet speed with the power of multiple connections
  • Back up connections for unobstructed live streaming even during connection failovers
  • Consistently high speed internet all throughout the stream, ensuring high quality streaming from start to end.

04. Rural Internet

Zifilink helps rural internet

A tuition center owner wanted to provide high speed internet to his students. Since his center was located in a Tier III city, broadband internet was not available.

Zifilink device helped a tuition center owner with setting up a reliable and high-speed internet in a Tier 3 city. The location was devoid of any proper broadband connection, which was hampering the productivity of his students and thus, slowed down the curriculum.

In a remote area where native lifestyles have been largely untouched with the most modern and latest technologies, bringing about a drastic change in the way education is provided to the promising minds of tomorrow was challenging and even worse, doubtful.

However, it appeared as though the tuition owner was determined to deliver education to all his students, unhindered. Up until our intervention, had used three different mobile carriers- BSNL, Jio, and Vodafone to get a stable internet connection. But this approach failed as the data speed did not help in securing the kind of reliable internet connection that’s needed in providing seamless online education.

Zifilink acknowledged this passion to teach and educate and stepped in to solve each of the prevailing issues with one solution- Zifilink 3X. With Zifilink 3X internet bonding router, the tutor was able to connect to and integrate all the three network providers, resulting in an output of an aggregate speed of 20 Mbps.

Challenges
  • The tuition owner had access to BSNL, Jio, and Vodafone sim cards, each of which had a data cap of 2 GB per day. This meant that classes couldn’t even get past half the curriculum in a day, which was a major concern for the tutor and the students alike.
  • The region in discussion is a remote location that did not have a basic broadband connection. In other words, there was no reliable source that could satisfy the needs of seamless online education in the region.
Solution

  • Zifilink deployed its 3X internet bonding device- a device that assembles and aggregates package chunks at our remote package assembler service, before transmitting it to the required destination. This ensures significantly high internet speeds, perfectly suited for online education in the remote region.

  • Zifilink automatically detects and dials the dongle, which ensured that the tutor didn’t have to manually switch between networks when one suffered a failover or reached its data cap. As a result, Zifilink 3X was able to foster seamless education in the area, which has continued unhindered ever since.
